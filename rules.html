Rules & Instructions

  • Register your team with a Team Name before starting.
  • Each team gets a unique question sequence. Do not follow other teams.
  • Solve the riddle → find the QR code on campus → scan it to unlock the next question.
  • QR scan only works for your team’s current question. Wrong scans show: "Wrong QR! Not unlocked."
  • The timer starts when you click Start the Hunt and stops after 6th Question
  • Show the final screen to organisers to record your time.
  • One device per team. Do not refresh, go back, or tamper with the app.
  • Respect campus rules and other participants.
  • Disqualification for skipping questions, scanning out of order, or damaging QR codes.
